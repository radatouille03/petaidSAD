What to do if your dog is choking
Dog Critical 3 min read Verified by vet
Choking in dogs is a life-threatening emergency. Act quickly but stay calm. Follow these steps immediately.
1
Stay calm and restrain your dog
A choking dog may panic and bite. Speak calmly and gently hold your dog still before attempting anything.
2
Check the mouth
Open the mouth carefully and look inside. If you can clearly see and safely reach the object, try to remove it with two fingers. Do not do a blind finger sweep.
3
Apply back blows
Tilt the dog forward and give up to 5 firm blows between the shoulder blades using the heel of your hand.
4
Apply abdominal thrusts
Stand behind your dog, make a fist and place it just below the rib cage. Pull sharply inward and upward up to 5 times.
5
Call a vet immediately
Even if the object is removed, take your dog to a vet right away to check for internal damage.
Warning
Never do a blind finger sweep — this can push the object further down the throat.
Applies to
All dog breeds and sizes. Adjust pressure based on the dog's size.

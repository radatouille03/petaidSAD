Rabbit seizure — immediate response
Rabbit Moderate 4 min read
A rabbit seizure can be frightening but knowing how to respond can help keep your rabbit safe. Follow these steps carefully.
1
Stay calm and do not restrain your rabbit. Restraining a seizing rabbit can cause injury.
2
Clear the area around the rabbit. Remove any hard or sharp objects that could cause injury during the seizure.
3
Time the seizure. Note when it started and how long it lasts — this is important information for your vet.
4
Keep the rabbit warm and dark. Place a soft cloth nearby but do not cover the rabbit's face.
5
Contact a vet immediately after the seizure ends, even if the rabbit appears to recover.
Warning
Never put anything in the rabbit's mouth during a seizure.
